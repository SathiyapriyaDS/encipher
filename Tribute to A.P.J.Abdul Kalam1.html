Tribute to A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

A.P.J Adbul Kalam,the "Missile Man Of india,"was born on October 15,1931,in Rameswaram, TamilNadu He served as the 11th President of india and was a reowned scientist, known for his worj in india's space and Missile programs.


Achievements
  • Developed india's first satellite launch vehicle(SLV-III)
  • Led india's successful nuclear test in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020"
  • Received the Bharat Ratna,India's highest civilian award
