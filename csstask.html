selection

computing and web design, selection usually refers to the portion of text or content that a user highlights with their mouse, trackpad, or keyboard.When you click and drag over text in a browser or document, that highlighted area is called the selection.The browser applies a default style to it (often blue background with white text).CSS gives us the ::selection pseudo-element to customize how that highlighted text looks.
